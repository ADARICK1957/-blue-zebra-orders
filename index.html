More than a packing list

A complete game-day preparation tool.

The Blue Zebra Officials Prep Card helps officials arrive prepared, stay organized, and build better game awareness. It combines equipment reminders, pregame mental preparation, game administration items, communication cues, and situational awareness prompts.

Uniform & ApparelShirt, pants, shoes, weather gear, backup items.
Core EquipmentWhistle, flags, bean bags, down indicator, coin, watch, pencil.
Pregame MentalArrive calm, know crew roles, review rules, communicate clearly.
Game AdminAssignment info, crew contacts, phone, badge, forms, clipboard.
Situational AwarenessDown and distance, clock, ball position, formations, keys, sidelines.
Emergency & Vehicle KitFirst aid, CPR mask, sanitizer, flashlight, backup uniform, water stash.
